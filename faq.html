Frequently Asked Questions

How to get to Scandinavia?

You can travel to Scandinavia by train, by car, by bus or by airplane.

What is the weather ike in Russia?

Russia consists of 11 time zones and over 15 climate types ranging from the Artic climate to Subtropical. Moscow is considered to have humid continental climate with warm and sometimes even hot summers.

What is the "White Nights"?

THe "White Night" phenomenon happens every year between May and mid-July in places close to the Artic Circle. During iths time, the sun never truly sets and the sky stays light for most of the night.

Where are the northern lights?

The closer to the Arctic circle you are the better you can see them.

What is an aurora?

Auroras are caused by electronically charged particles originating from the sun. Multi-colored displays form when different atmospheric gases are agitated by this solar wind.
